About Us

MABUHAY POWER & DIESEL GENERATOR SERVICES CO., LTD. was organized on 27 July 2012 in Carmona, Cavite by four distinct people who are creative, hardworking, trustworthy, and dedicated to their works and responsibilities. They are committed in their intension to serve generators engine to the best that they can. The organizers were former employees and staff of Cummins Power Generator.

MABUHAY POWER & DIESEL GENERATOR SERVICES CO., LTD. extends their services further, not only in selling Cummins and other brands of generator to our valued customers, but also offering the best quality services for repair and maintenance. We offer and repair other brand of generators with lesser cost but with world class standard.

Our Objective

To contribute unequalled quality of products and services to the users of generators and help our country in industrialization using modern technology.

Our Mission

Mabuhay Power supplies the power needs of our clients through our various offered products and maintenance services. We focus our energy and dedication on ensuring that our client's businesses are well-protected. We seek to provide the best value to our customers, grow a profitable business, and provide job opportunities for our fellow countrymen.

Our Vision

Mabuhay Power aims to be the leading generator service provider in the country by constantly innovating our approach and advancing our technologies in the quest to deliver the best customer service.
