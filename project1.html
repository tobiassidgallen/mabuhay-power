2 UNITS 2MVA CATERPILLAR GENERATOR SET SYNCHRONIZATION USING DEEPSEA 8610 ENGINE CONTROL MODULE

SAN MIGUEL CORPORATION MABINI, BATANGAS, PHILIPPINES

Project Overview

This project involved the synchronization of two 2MVA Caterpillar generator sets using the Deepsea 8610 Engine Control Module. The project was undertaken to address several critical issues affecting the generator sets' performance and reliability.

Challenges Encountered

During the initial assessment, we identified several significant issues:

  • Brownish deposits in the fuel tank
  • Contaminated engine oil with black residue particles
  • Excessive oil leaks from the crankshaft rear oil seal
  • Presence of "Diesel Fuel Bugs" in the Racor Element

Solutions Implemented

Our comprehensive solution included:

  • Complete fuel tank cleaning and maintenance
  • Racor assembly cleaning and optimization
  • Engine lubricating oil and filter replacement
  • PT pump repair and actuator installation
  • Injector repair and installation
  • Complete engine tune-up
  • Radiator assembly maintenance and belt installation
  • ECM & EFC installation and configuration
